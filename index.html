leeHom

Bayesian reconstruction of ancient DNA fragments

View project on GitHub

About

leeHom is a Bayesian maximum a posteriori algorithm for stripping sequencing adapters and merging overlapping portions of reads. Our algorithm is moslty aimed at ancient DNA but can be used for any dataset. For optimal performance, users can optionally enter a prior that models de probability of observing a specific insert size.

Citing

leeHom: adaptor trimming and merging for Illumina sequencing reads
Gabriel Renaud, Udo Stenzel and Janet Kelso
Nucleic Acids Res. 2014 Oct;42(18):e141. doi: 10.1093/nar/gku699.
